Biography

LeBron Raymone James Sr. is an American professional basketball player for the Los Angeles Lakers of the National Basketball Association (NBA). Often considered one of the greatest NBA players in history, he is frequently compared to Michael Jordan in debates over the greatest basketball player ever.
